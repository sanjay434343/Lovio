🏭 Go visit Elgi Rubber, Coimbatore

They are just 40 km away from Pollachi. They make small-scale retreading machines specifically for shops like yours, give training, and even have an after-sales team nearby. This is Pollachi's biggest advantage over shops in other towns.

💰 Apply for PMEGP loan

District Industries Centre (DIC) in Coimbatore offers PMEGP and CGTMSE-backed loans. The government funds 25–35% of your setup cost at subsidized interest. That means ₹2–4 lakh comes from the government, not from your pocket.

✅ Before spending any money...

Talk to 3 lorry owners you already know. Get a soft "yes, I'll bring my tires." If you can't get even 3 committed customers, don't open yet. Real market validation = real lorry owners saying yes, not just a plan on paper.

📍 Where to set up in Pollachi

Best locations: near NH-544 corridor, near the APMC market area, or near the Pollachi transport nagar. High lorry traffic, easy for customers to find you, and shed rent is lower than in town.

Section 04 — Customers

Who to get as customers in Pollachi

Focus on these four types. Lorry owners are your bread and butter. Tractor owners are your backup in farm season.

01
35–40% of business

Private Lorry Owners (NH-544)

Lorries on the Pollachi–Coimbatore–Kerala corridor. They save ₹10,000–15,000 per tire by retreading instead of buying new. Very loyal if you're reliable and fast. Target 4–5 lorry owners with 2–5 trucks each as regulars.

02
20–25% of business

Tractor Owners (Coconut & Banana Belt)

Pollachi is surrounded by farms. Tractor rear tires cost ₹15,000+ new. Your retread: ₹3,000–4,500. Huge saving. Peak demand Jan–Feb (post-harvest) and Sep–Oct (harvest prep). Great seasonal filler.

03
15–20% of business

Mini-Truck & Tempo Owners (APMC Market)

Pollachi's banana and coconut APMC yard has dozens of mini-trucks running daily. Lower margin per tire than lorries, but good steady volume and these owners are close by — easy pickup and delivery.

04
10–15% of business

Valparai & Anamalai Jeeps and Buses

Tourism jeeps and private mini-buses going up to Valparai have extreme tire wear due to the ghat road. They need retreading frequently and are loyal to a good shop. Niche but profitable. Good margin tires.

Section 05 — Risks

What can go wrong & how to prevent it

These are the real causes of retreading shops failing. Know them, prepare for them.

📉 Volume drops below 5 tires/day

Fix: Pre-sign 2–3 lorry owners as regulars before you even open. Like a tiffin center locking in office clients before cooking starts. No customers = don't open yet.

📈 Rubber price shoots up (2024: +50%)

Fix: Buy 2–3 months rubber stock in March–May when prices are low. Store it. Saves ₹15,000–25,000 when prices spike mid-year.

💸 Customer doesn't pay on time

Fix: New customers — cash only. Old regulars — 30 days max credit. Never let one person owe you more than ₹30,000. Like a grocery shop policy.

🔧 Buffing machine breaks down

Fix: Get annual maintenance contract with Elgi Rubber CBE. Keep spare rasps and belts in stock always. Machine downtime = zero income those days.

⚡ Power cut during curing

Fix: A mid-cure power failure ruins the tire and wastes material. Keep a small diesel genset (₹30,000–40,000) as backup. Worth every rupee.

🗂️ Bad casings getting accepted

Fix: You (or your best person) must inspect every casing before starting work. A rejected casing accepted by mistake wastes all rubber and labor spent on it.
Section 06 — Growth

Simple ways to grow your shop

These cost almost nothing but can double your customer base within a year.

🚛

Free pickup service

Collect and deliver tires within 15 km of Pollachi at no charge. Lorry owners love this convenience. Zero cost to you, wins 3–5 big accounts quickly without advertising.

🛡️

Offer a small warranty

30,000 km mileage guarantee per retread. Nobody else in Pollachi does this. Charge ₹200 extra per tire — customers gladly pay. Builds enormous trust and repeat business.

📱

WhatsApp mileage reminders

"Your Ashok Leyland did 70,000 km — time to retread?" Send this message monthly. Zero cost. Works brilliantly in Tamil Nadu's commercial vehicle community where everyone is on WhatsApp.

🤝

Join Pollachi Lorry Union

Pollachi has active lorry owner associations. Become their recommended shop — 30–50 lorries automatically become your customers. One relationship replaces months of marketing.

🌞

Target tractors in June–July

When lorry business slows in monsoon, farmers are preparing land after rain. Tractor tire retreading partially fills the gap. Keep tractor contacts warm all year for this.

⭐

Get BIS certification (Year 2)

BIS IS:15626 certification for retreading unlocks TNSTC bus contracts — government bulk orders. Rare among Pollachi competitors and commands 15% pricing premium.
